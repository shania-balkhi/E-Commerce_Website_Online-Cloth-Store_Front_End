Our Services

Made To Measure

Once your order details are received a fitting prototype in cotton is developed from scratch based on your measurements. We will make necessary adjustment to your pattern and proceed. If we feel second fitting is require we will send you second fitting prototype. This is the reason number of measurements we request are slightly higher than normal.

Bulk
Ordering

We know how bulk ordering can be quite a task but worry not, we understand all your shopping needs & strive to meet them as effectively and conveniently as possible. Great prices, no anxiety shipping and hassle-free returns, we are the one-stop shop for all your bulk/customized orders. We manage your bulk orders with a single P.O, payment, checkout, and delivery.

Easy
Alterations

When your most loved garment does not fit you anymore you can always send it to us for alteration. It doesn't have to be a garment bought at our site. We will send you second fitting prototype. This is the reason number of measurements we request are slightly higher than normal. We will make adjustment to your pattern and proceed with the alteration.
